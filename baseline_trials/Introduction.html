Bonus points

{{ endblock }} {{ block content }}

Throughout the study, you will be paired with other participants who are also taking part in the study.

In addition to the Toluna points you receive for participating, you can receive bonus points based on your own and other participants' decisions. At the end of the study, the bonus points you collected will be converted into Toluna points at the following conversion rate:

1 point = {{ session.real_world_currency_per_point }} Toluna points

You will make decisions in different situations and in different roles. At the end of the study, one situation and one role will be randomly selected to determine your bonus points. Since you will not know in advance which decision will count, you should treat each decision as if it is selected.

Bonus points are calculated once all participants have completed the study.

Please read the instructions carefully, as your decisions affect not only your own Toluna points but also those of other participants.
