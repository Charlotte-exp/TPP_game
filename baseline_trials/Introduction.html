Throughout the study, you will be paired with other participants who are also taking part in the study.

In addition to the compensation you receive for participating, you can receive bonus points based on your own and other participants' decisions.

1 point = {{ session.real_world_currency_per_point }} GBP

You will make decisions in different situations and in different roles. At the end of the study, one situation and one role will be randomly selected to determine your bonus points. Since you will not know in advance which decision will count, you should treat each decision as if it is selected.

Block 1

There are two blocks in this study: a longer first block and a shorter second block. In the first block, you will make decisions in different situations and in different roles. After the first block of decisions, one situation and one role from the first block will be randomly selected to determine your bonus points in the first block.

Block 2

In the second block, you will make additional decisions in different situations, using your bonus points from the first block. After the second block, again one situation will be randomly selected to determine your final bonus points.

After the study

Bonus points are calculated after all participants have completed the study. They cannot be negative. Since you will not know in advance which decisions will count, you should treat each decision as if it is selected.

Please read the instructions carefully, as your decisions affect not only your payoff but also those of other participants. {{ endif }}
